How to Donate

Donating blood is simple and takes only a few minutes. Here's how you can help:

  • Visit one of our donation centers or mobile blood drives.
  • Fill out a short health questionnaire.
  • Donate blood with the help of our trained medical staff.
  • Enjoy some refreshments to help you recover after donating.
